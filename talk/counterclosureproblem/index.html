Why is it that, if Counter-Closure is Accepted, There is a Problem for Inferential Knowledge?

Abstract

This poster addresses a case of underdetermination in regards to the DeBrogile-Bohm and Many Worlds interpretations of quantum mechanics. I show that cases of underdetermination of theories that make differing ontological posits casts doubt on several proposed solutions to the problem of epistemic counter-closure.
